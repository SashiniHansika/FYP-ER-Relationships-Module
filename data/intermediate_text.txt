for each drug, the trade name and formula must be recorded.

every patient has a primary physician.

each pharmacy sells several drugs and has a price for each.
a drug could be sold at several pharmacies, and a price for each could vary from one pharmacy to another.

each prescription has a date and a quantity associated with each prescription.
